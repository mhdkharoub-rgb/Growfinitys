Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026 · Effective immediately
GrowFinitys ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our Pi Network application available at growfinitys.pi.

1. Information We Collect

When you use GrowFinitys, we may collect the following information:

  • Pi Network UID: Your unique Pi Network user identifier, provided when you authenticate via the Pi SDK. This is used to manage your subscription.
  • Pi Username: Your Pi Network username, used to personalise your experience within the app.
  • Payment Information: Payment IDs and transaction IDs generated by the Pi Network payment system. We do not store your wallet address or private keys.
  • Subscription Data: Your chosen plan (Starter, Pro, or Annual), activation date, and expiry date.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with the app, such as which signal categories you view most often.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate your identity via Pi Network and grant access to member features
  • Process and verify your Pi payment and activate your subscription
  • Deliver AI-generated trading signals appropriate to your subscription tier
  • Send important service updates or subscription renewal reminders
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of our signal generation
  • Prevent fraud and abuse of the platform

3. Trading Signals Disclaimer

All trading signals provided by GrowFinitys are generated by artificial intelligence for informational purposes only. They do not constitute financial advice, investment recommendations, or solicitation to buy or sell any asset. Trading involves significant risk of loss. Always conduct your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making any trading decisions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited cases:

  • Pi Network: Payment verification is processed through the Pi Network Server API. Pi Network's own privacy policy applies to data processed on their platform.
  • Anthropic: Signal generation queries are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. No personally identifiable information is included in these queries.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our backend servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS). We retain your subscription data for as long as your account is active, and for up to 12 months after your last subscription expires for record-keeping purposes.

We do not store sensitive financial information. All payment processing is handled entirely by Pi Network's infrastructure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Cookies & Tracking

GrowFinitys does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts. We may use minimal session storage within the app to maintain your login state during a session. No data is shared with advertisers.

8. Children's Privacy

GrowFinitys is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
